## About

Avior is the Lead Developer of nxtdev.xyz, responsible for the architecture, infrastructure, and ongoing development of the platform. With over three years of experience in full-stack development and a specialisation in developer tooling, automation, and self-hosted infrastructure, he oversees the technical direction of the project from the ground up.

His work on nxtdev.xyz spans the full stack — from DNS infrastructure and subdomain registry logic to the public-facing platform and documentation system. He brings a strong background in modern web technologies including Next.js, TypeScript, and Node.js, alongside hands-on expertise in DevOps, CI/CD pipelines, and cloud infrastructure.

Beyond nxtdev.xyz, Avior maintains an independent network of personal projects and services under the `avrxt.in` domain, with a focus on building reliable, developer-first tools.
