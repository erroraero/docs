If you find a nxtdev.xyz subdomain being used for phishing, spam, malware, or any other form of abuse, you can file a report directly from the platform. Reports are reviewed by the moderation team within 24 hours, and subdomains confirmed to be in active abuse may be suspended sooner.

## Filing an abuse report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the report page">
    Navigate to [nxtdev.xyz/report](https://nxtdev.xyz/report). You do not need to be logged in to submit a report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the subdomain">
    Type the full subdomain you are reporting (for example, `suspicious.nxtdev.xyz`). You can also paste the full URL — the form will extract the subdomain automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a category">
    Choose the category that best describes the abuse:

    - **Phishing** — credential harvesting or impersonation of a brand
    - **Malware** — hosting or distributing malicious software
    - **Spam** — unsolicited bulk messaging or redirect spam
    - **Scam/Fraud** — financial scams or deceptive schemes
    - **Copyright** — unauthorized use of copyrighted material
    - **Other** — anything that does not fit the above categories
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add details and evidence">
    Describe what you observed in the details field. Include any relevant context such as screenshots, URLs of specific pages, or dates when the activity occurred. The more evidence you provide, the faster the team can act.

    You may also include your email address if you want to be notified when the report is resolved. This field is optional.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the report">
    Click **Submit**. You will see a confirmation message on the page. If the subdomain is in active abuse, the team may suspend it before the standard 24-hour review window.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you have multiple pieces of evidence — for example, a screenshot and a network log — paste them all into the details field or include links to where they are hosted. The moderation team reviews everything you submit.
</Tip>

## Report by email

If you prefer not to use the form, you can send abuse reports directly to [abuse@nxtdev.xyz](mailto:abuse@nxtdev.xyz). Include the full subdomain name, the category of abuse, and any supporting evidence in your message.

<Note>
  Reports sent by email go through the same review process as form submissions. Response times are the same.
</Note>

## Appealing a suspension

If your own subdomain was suspended and you believe the decision was made in error, contact [support@nxtdev.xyz](mailto:support@nxtdev.xyz) with the following information:

- Your account email address
- The full subdomain name (for example, `myproject.nxtdev.xyz`)
- A brief description of what your subdomain is used for
- Any context that explains why the suspension may have been triggered incorrectly

The support team will review your case and respond within 24 hours on business days.

<Warning>
  While a subdomain is suspended, DNS record changes and delegation changes are blocked. Your subdomain is read-only until the suspension is lifted. Do not create a new subdomain with the same label as a workaround — contact support directly instead.
</Warning>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens after I submit a report?">
    Your report is assigned an internal ID and enters the moderation queue with a status of **open**. A moderator reviews it and takes action — either suspending the subdomain, dismissing the report, or requesting more information. If the subdomain is confirmed to be in active abuse, it may be suspended before the 24-hour window.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will I be notified when my report is resolved?">
    If you provided your email address when submitting the report, the team may follow up with you. Resolution status is not automatically emailed, but you can check by emailing [abuse@nxtdev.xyz](mailto:abuse@nxtdev.xyz) and referencing the subdomain you reported.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I report a subdomain that is not on nxtdev.xyz?">
    No. The report form only accepts subdomains under `nxtdev.xyz`. For abuse on other domains, contact that domain's registrar or hosting provider.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
